Naturely.


A fully functional React Native full stack mobile application, using a Firebase back-end including Firebase authenication and storage. This was part of our Northcoders team project phase. The premise behind this app was a social media and photo sharing app specifically for wildlife and nature. The USP of this was that it is map based, so you can see where posts have been made with ease and then go out with hopes of seeing similar picturesque spots. It allows posting with photos, topic tags, geo-tags and descriptions. You can also comment on users posts, filter posts, add new topics, view profiles, and look around on map view.
